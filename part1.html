IELTS Speaking — Part 1 (50 Questions)

Click a question to reveal a Band-9 sample answer and useful phrases.

1. Do you like your hometown?

Sample: Yes, I do. My hometown combines modern conveniences with a strong sense of community, which makes it comfortable to live in. I particularly appreciate the local markets and friendly neighbours.

Useful: "strong sense of community", "modern conveniences", "particularly appreciate"
2. What do you usually do in your free time?

Sample: In my free time I like to read and go for walks. Reading relaxes me and helps me learn new things, while walking clears my head and keeps me fit.

Useful: "in my free time", "clears my head", "keeps me fit"
3. Do you prefer tea or coffee?

Sample: I prefer coffee because it gives me an energy boost in the morning and helps me concentrate. Occasionally I enjoy herbal tea in the evening to relax.

Useful: "energy boost", "helps me concentrate", "occasionally enjoy"
4. How often do you use social media?

Sample: I use social media moderately — mainly to keep in touch with friends and follow news. I try to avoid endless scrolling and focus on useful content instead.

Useful: "keep in touch", "follow news", "avoid endless scrolling"
5. Do you enjoy cooking?

Sample: Yes, I enjoy cooking because it lets me be creative and share meals with family. Trying new recipes is fun and rewarding when guests compliment the food.

Useful: "be creative", "share meals", "trying new recipes"
6. How do you usually travel to work or school?

Sample: I usually take public transport because it’s convenient and eco-friendly. During the commute I often read or listen to podcasts to make the time productive.

Useful: "public transport", "eco-friendly", "make the time productive"
7. Do you like to watch films at home or at the cinema?

Sample: I prefer watching films at the cinema for big blockbusters because the big screen and sound system enhance the experience. For documentaries or dramas I usually watch at home.

Useful: "enhance the experience", "blockbusters", "documentaries or dramas"
8. What kinds of books do you like?

Sample: I enjoy a mix of fiction and non-fiction; novels for imagination and biographies or essays for ideas. Non-fiction helps me learn practical insights I can apply in life.

Useful: "a mix of", "practical insights", "apply in life"
9. Do you play any sports?

Sample: I play football occasionally with friends and go jogging a few times a week to stay fit. Team sports are great for socialising, while jogging is good for clear thinking.

Useful: "stay fit", "socialising", "clear thinking"
10. How often do you meet your friends?

Sample: I try to meet close friends at least once a week, usually for coffee or a meal. Regular meetups keep relationships strong despite busy schedules.

Useful: "at least once a week", "keep relationships strong", "busy schedules"
11. Do you prefer the countryside or the city?

Sample: I appreciate both but prefer living in the city for convenience and opportunities. I visit the countryside for relaxation and to enjoy nature on weekends.

Useful: "appreciate both", "convenience and opportunities", "enjoy nature"
12. What was your favourite subject at school?

Sample: My favourite subject was literature because I loved analysing stories and exploring different perspectives. It helped me develop critical thinking and empathy.

Useful: "analysing stories", "different perspectives", "critical thinking"
13. Do you like shopping for clothes?

Sample: I enjoy shopping occasionally, especially when looking for quality items. I prefer planning purchases rather than impulse buying to ensure value for money.

Useful: "quality items", "planning purchases", "value for money"
14. Do you have any pets?

Sample: Yes, I have a dog which is part of the family. Taking care of a pet teaches responsibility and brings a lot of joy to daily life.

Useful: "part of the family", "teaches responsibility", "brings joy"
15. How do you usually celebrate special occasions?

Sample: I usually celebrate with family meals and small gatherings; sharing food and stories makes occasions memorable. Occasionally we travel to mark special milestones.

Useful: "family meals", "memorable occasions", "mark special milestones"
16. Do you like to take photos?

Sample: Yes, photography helps me capture moments and notice details I might otherwise miss. I enjoy taking informal photos when I travel or meet friends.

Useful: "capture moments", "notice details", "when I travel"
17. Do you prefer to eat at home or in restaurants?

Sample: I prefer eating at home for everyday meals because it’s healthier and more economical, but I enjoy restaurants for special occasions or trying new cuisines.

Useful: "healthier", "more economical", "trying new cuisines"
18. How often do you exercise?

Sample: I try to exercise three times a week, usually jogging or doing a short gym session. Regular exercise helps maintain energy and reduces stress.

Useful: "three times a week", "maintain energy", "reduces stress"
19. What apps do you use most on your phone?

Sample: I mainly use messaging, note-taking and language learning apps. They help me stay organised, communicate with others and improve skills efficiently.

Useful: "stay organised", "language learning apps", "improve skills"
20. Do you like to travel by plane?

Sample: I don’t mind flying; it’s fast and convenient for long distances. However, I prefer scenic trains for shorter trips if time allows.

Useful: "fast and convenient", "scenic trains", "if time allows"
21. How important is punctuality to you?

Sample: Punctuality is very important as it shows respect for others’ time and helps plans run smoothly. I try to arrive a little early for appointments.

Useful: "shows respect", "run smoothly", "arrive a little early"
22. Do you prefer to plan or be spontaneous?

Sample: I usually plan important tasks to avoid stress but leave room for spontaneity in leisure activities. A balance of both works best for me.

Useful: "avoid stress", "leave room", "a balance of both"
23. How do you relax after a busy day?

Sample: I relax by reading, listening to music or taking a short walk. These simple activities help me unwind and recharge for the next day.

Useful: "unwind", "recharge", "simple activities"
24. Do you like meeting new people?

Sample: Yes, meeting new people is stimulating and broadens my perspective. While I may be reserved at first, meaningful conversations often follow.

Useful: "broadens my perspective", "reserved at first", "meaningful conversations"
25. What is your favourite season?

Sample: My favourite season is spring because the weather is mild and nature renews itself. It’s an ideal time for outdoor activities and new beginnings.

Useful: "mild weather", "nature renews itself", "new beginnings"
26. Do you prefer to read online or on paper?

Sample: I use both: online for quick articles and paper for in-depth reading. Paper helps me focus better without digital distractions.

Useful: "in-depth reading", "digital distractions", "use both"
27. How often do you visit your relatives?

Sample: I visit relatives on major occasions and try to see close family more regularly. Maintaining these ties is important for mutual support and tradition.

Useful: "major occasions", "mutual support", "maintaining ties"
28. Do you like to cook traditional dishes?

Sample: Yes, cooking traditional dishes connects me to my culture and family memories. I enjoy learning recipes passed down through generations.

Useful: "connects me to my culture", "family memories", "passed down"
29. What would you do on a free day?

Sample: On a free day I like to mix relaxation with light activity — reading, meeting friends or going for a hike. These activities help me recharge and pursue hobbies.

Useful: "mix relaxation with activity", "recharge", "pursue hobbies"
30. Do you prefer watching sports or playing them?

Sample: I prefer playing sports because it’s good exercise and social; watching is enjoyable when there’s a major event or to support friends.

Useful: "good exercise", "support friends", "major event"
31. Do you like art galleries?

Sample: I enjoy art galleries because they offer a quiet space to reflect and encounter different ideas. Exhibitions often inspire creativity and fresh thinking.

Useful: "offer a quiet space", "encounter ideas", "inspire creativity"
32. How often do you buy new clothes?

Sample: I buy new clothes occasionally and prefer quality over quantity. I plan purchases carefully to ensure they suit my needs and style.

Useful: "quality over quantity", "plan purchases", "suit my needs"
33. Do you enjoy gardening?

Sample: I enjoy simple gardening like growing herbs or flowers; it’s relaxing and rewarding to see growth over time. Gardening also teaches patience and care.

Useful: "growing herbs", "relaxing and rewarding", "teaches patience"
34. What do you do to stay healthy?

Sample: I combine regular exercise, balanced diet and adequate sleep to stay healthy. Small daily habits like walking and mindful eating add up over time.

Useful: "balanced diet", "adequate sleep", "small daily habits"
35. Do you follow fashion trends?

Sample: I follow trends casually but prefer clothes that are comfortable and timeless. I adapt trends to suit my personal style rather than follow them blindly.

Useful: "follow casually", "timeless", "personal style"
36. Do you like to cook for others?

Sample: I like cooking for others because it’s a way to show care and share culture. Preparing a meal for friends always feels rewarding.

Useful: "show care", "share culture", "feels rewarding"
37. How do you usually celebrate birthdays?

Sample: I celebrate birthdays with close family and friends, often sharing a meal and exchanging small gifts. It’s usually low-key but meaningful.

Useful: "close family and friends", "low-key but meaningful", "sharing a meal"
38. Do you prefer to study alone or in groups?

Sample: I prefer a mix: studying alone for concentration and group sessions for discussion and clarification. Both approaches complement each other well.

Useful: "concentration", "group sessions", "complement each other"
39. How important is money to you?

Sample: Money is important as it provides security and enables opportunities, but it’s not the sole measure of success. I value personal growth and relationships equally.

Useful: "provides security", "not the sole measure", "personal growth"
40. Do you prefer listening to radio or podcasts?

Sample: I prefer podcasts because I can choose topics that interest me and listen on demand. They are convenient for learning during commutes or chores.

Useful: "on demand", "during commutes", "choose topics"
41. What kind of weather do you like?

Sample: I like mild, sunny weather because it encourages outdoor activities and boosts mood. Extreme weather tends to limit plans.

Useful: "mild, sunny", "encourages outdoor activities", "boosts mood"
42. Do you prefer to keep a diary or notes?

Sample: I keep notes rather than a diary; short lists and reminders help me stay organised. Writing things down makes tasks easier to manage.

Useful: "stay organised", "writing things down", "manage tasks"
43. How often do you use a computer?

Sample: I use a computer daily for study, work and entertainment. Good digital skills are essential in modern life, so I practise regularly.

Useful: "daily for study", "digital skills", "practise regularly"
44. Do you like going to cafes?

Sample: I enjoy cafes occasionally as they provide a relaxed space to work or meet friends. A quiet café can be very productive for focused tasks.

Useful: "relaxed space", "meet friends", "productive"
45. What do you usually buy at the supermarket?

Sample: I mainly buy fresh produce and staples, planning meals ahead to avoid waste. I also look for seasonal items for better flavour and value.

Useful: "fresh produce", "plan meals", "seasonal items"
46. Do you like visiting museums?

Sample: Yes, museums are great places to learn about history and culture. I find well-curated exhibitions both educational and inspiring.

Useful: "well-curated exhibitions", "educational and inspiring", "learn about history"
47. How do you keep up with current events?

Sample: I follow reputable news sites and a few daily podcasts; I prefer concise summaries that give me a clear picture of major developments. I also cross-check stories when they seem surprising.

Useful: "reputable news sites", "concise summaries", "cross-check stories"
48. What is your favourite way to travel long distances?

Sample: For long distances I prefer trains when time allows because they are scenic and comfortable; for urgent trips, flights are more practical.

Useful: "time allows", "scenic and comfortable", "more practical"
49. Do you like giving or receiving gifts?

Sample: I enjoy giving gifts because choosing something meaningful for someone is rewarding. Receiving gifts is lovely too, especially when it reflects thoughtfulness.

Useful: "meaningful", "rewarding", "thoughtfulness"
50. What helps you learn better?

Sample: Active practice, structured routines and timely feedback help me learn best. Setting small goals and reviewing material regularly reinforce memory and progress.

Useful: "active practice", "structured routines", "timely feedback"
Tip: practice saying each answer aloud for 30–90 seconds and personalise details to sound natural.
